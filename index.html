Credentialing built into the pipeline

Primary-source license checks, expiration tracking and automated re-verification. Candidates upload once and Fonnil keeps the documents current from application through employment.

  • Dashboard verification for 50 states
  • OIG / NPI exclusion screening
  • Expiration alerts before they lapse

Trained to find the best candidate — for every role

Fonnil scores every candidate on what actually predicts success in that role — not keyword matching. And you set the bar: define the exact profile, skills and certifications you want for any seat in the practice. Fonnil shows its work on every score.

  • Experience, licensure & certifications, diagnosis-aware
  • Clinical & participation-weighted, role-adjacent
  • Custom profiles per role — clinical, admin or ops
  • Every score explainable, ranked and testable
